### Ride Booking Performance Revenue Analytics Dashboard Using Power BI

# Business Insights & Strategic Recommendations:-

# Executive Summary:-
This project analyzes **200,000+ Ride Booking Records** to evaluate **Revenue Performance, Customer Behavior, Geographic Trends, and Operational Efficiency** for a ride-hailing business.  
The analysis identifies **Revenue Leakage Due To Cancellations**, **City-Wise Performance Gaps**, and **Vehicle-Type Profitability Differences**, enabling **Data-Driven Strategic Decisions**.
The dataset was cleaned and transformed using **Power Query, Including Handling Missing Values, Standardizing Categories, and Optimizing The Data Model**.

-----

# Revenue Insights:-
- Total revenue is **Highly Concentrated**, with **Top 3 Cities Contributing ~50%** of overall revenue.
- **Sedan and SUV Categories** generate **Higher Average Booking Value** compared to bikes.
- A significant number of bookings have **Low Ride Value**, impacting profitability despite high volume.

**Insight:-** Increasing focus on **High-Value Rides and Premium Vehicle Segments** can substantially improve total revenue without increasing booking volume.

-----

# Geographic Insights:-
- Certain cities show **High Booking Volume But Low Revenue**, indicating short-distance or discounted rides.
- Few cities consistently experience **Higher Cancellation Rates**.
- Cities with **Better Driver Ratings** demonstrate stronger customer retention.

**Insight:-** City-Level Optimization is critical; a one-size-fits-all strategy reduces efficiency.

-----

# Operational Insights:-
- **Driver Unavailability and Long Wait Times** are the top reasons for cancellations.
- Cancellation spikes during **Peak Hours and Weekends**.
- Premium vehicle categories show **Lower Cancellation Rates and Higher Completion Ratios**.

**Insight:-** Operational Efficiency (driver supply & response time) has a direct impact on revenue and customer satisfaction.

-----

# Customer Behavior Insights:-
- Customers with ratings below 3.5 show **~20–25% Higher Cancellation Frequency**.
- Repeat cancellations correlate with **Long Wait Times and Low Driver Ratings**.

**Insight:-** Improving service quality directly impacts **customer retention and ride completion**.

-----

# Business Challenges Identified:-
- High Cancellation Rate causing **Direct Revenue Leakage**.
- Uneven City-Wise Revenue Performance.
- **Underutilization of Premium Vehicle Categories**.
- Inconsistent driver performance affecting ratings.
- Peak-Hour demand–supply imbalance.

-----

# Strategic Recommendations:-
- Optimize Driver Allocation during Peak Hours.
- Increase incentives for High-Rated Drivers.
- Promote Premium Vehicle Categories during High-Demand Periods.
- Reduce wait times in High-Cancellation Cities.
- Monitor City-Wise KPIs for targeted Decision-Making.

-----

# Expected Business Impact:-
- **5–8% Increase in Ride Completion Rate**.
- **7–10% Improvement in Total Revenue**.
- Reduced customer churn.
- **Better Driver Utilization and Satisfaction**.
- **Improved Operational Planning and Scalability**.

-----

## Conclusion:
This Power BI Dashboard demonstrates how **Data Analytics can directly Support Business Growth** by identifying Revenue Opportunities, Improving Operational Efficiency, and Enhancing Customer Experience.  
The project reflects **Real-World Decision-Making Scenarios** faced by ride-hailing companies like **Ola and Uber**, making it highly relevant for **Data Analyst roles**.
